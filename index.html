The Command Center

Wyo-Hazard is the premier FIRST Robotics Competition team representing Casper, Wyoming. Since 2004, Team 1303 has been a staple in the Wyoming STEM community, bringing together the brightest young minds to solve complex engineering problems.

Our workshop is a hub for mechanical design, electrical integration, and advanced programming. We don't just build robots; we build leaders who are ready to enter the workforce with real-world technical skills.
